Our Story

LensPro was born from a simple belief: that every event, whether intimate or grand, deserves to be captured with heart. What started as two friends with a camera and a dream has grown into a full-service studio trusted by hundreds of clients worldwide.

We've documented weddings across 12 countries, partnered with Fortune 500 companies, and captured the raw energy of music festivals — all while staying true to our roots: authentic, emotion-driven photography that never feels staged.

2018 — Founded in New York
2020 — Expanded to 15+ team members
2023 — Global coverage in 20+ cities
2025 — Best Event Photography Studio
